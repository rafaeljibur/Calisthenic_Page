What is calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a system of physical exercise where you only need your own body weight. It is a procedure of several exercises for a multitude of muscles and joints, routines that you can do at home, without the need for any specific equipment.

In the ancient world, calisthenics was used as the main source of physical preparation for the military, as it was easy to organize, easy to learn, and had the greatest transfer to actual skills and movements that soldiers would need in combat. Something that really remains today for its practicality and contribution to the integral physical power of the troops combined with the aforementioned benefits.

The Best Calisthenics Athletes

Hannibal For King

Age - 43 years (1978) EEUU

His real name is Hannibal Lanham and he was born in Jamaica Queens, New York, his childhood was not easy since his father, when he was very young, was arrested for some very serious crime. Hannibal For King was the inspiration of many calisthenics athletes, Hannibal showed an unprecedented control of his body with controlled and explosive movements that at that time we did not even know what they were called.

Daniels Lainzans

Age - 20 years (2000) Letonia

Daniels started in the Street Workout back in 2013, but he commented in one of his last interviews that he had previous experience in sports gymnastics training back in 2008. Daniels is a very complete athlete, both in static and dynamic exercises. capable of practically perfect planks and the most impressive freestyle combos.Without a doubt, he is the champion because of his great versatility, he is good at everything.

Andrea Larosa

Age - 24 years (1995) Italia

Larosa is one of the athletes who has managed to sell his image in a spectacular way, he has an incredible physique and one of the most aesthetic of the street workout. Larosa has been able to win 6 national and international streetworkout competitions. Andrea Larosa has not only been successful on social media, but has also been able to compete at the highest level in the various streetworkout competitions.
